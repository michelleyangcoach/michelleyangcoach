# Coaching

## My Approach: A Simple 3-Step Cycle

### 1. Self-Discovery  
Understand your thinking patterns, values, strengths, and goals.

### 2. Mapping Your Growth Areas  
Identify the relationships or situations you want to improve.

### 3. Your Clear Ask  
Articulate boundaries, expectations, and needs with clarity.

## What Coaching Looks Like

- Weekly or biweekly sessions  
- A safe, grounded space  
- Emotional & cultural insight  
- Tools you can use immediately  

## Who This Is For

- People struggling with difficult conversations  
- Individuals navigating cultural or identity transitions  
- Anyone seeking clearer communication or emotional alignment
